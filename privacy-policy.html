Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Music and Mastery respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This SMS Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you opt in to receive SMS communications from us.

2. Information We Collect

When you opt in to receive SMS messages from us, we may collect the following information:

  • Your phone number
  • Any information you provide in response to SMS messages
  • Message delivery and interaction data (such as whether a message was opened or clicked)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected through SMS messages for the following purposes:

  • To send service updates, promotional offers, and important notifications
  • To respond to customer inquiries and provide support
  • To improve our messaging services and enhance user experience
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your SMS-related information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • With service providers who assist us in delivering SMS services, provided they adhere to confidentiality agreements
  • In case of a business transfer, merger, or acquisition, where your information may be transferred as part of the assets

5. Opting Out

You can opt out of receiving SMS messages from us at any time by replying "STOP" to any message you receive. Once you opt out, you will no longer receive SMS communications from us unless you re-enroll.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no data transmission over mobile networks can be guaranteed as 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our SMS services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a minor, we will take steps to delete it.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this SMS Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on our website with the revised effective date.
